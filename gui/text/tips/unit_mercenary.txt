MERCENARIES

• Enabled in town phase.

• Can not gather resources.

• Cost silver instead of other resources:
    • Infantry: 60 silver
    • Camel: 75 silver
    • Cavalry: 90 silver
    • Biga: 180 silver
    • Quadriga: 240 silver
    • Elephant: 300 silver

• Slightly more powerful:
    • −50% training time
    • +10% health
    • +20% capture attack strength
    • +15% attack damage
